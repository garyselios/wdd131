Our Story Among the Trees

Discover the history, people, and passion behind Inn Among Trees

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2005 by nature enthusiasts Michael and Paul Rogers, Inn Among Trees began as a dream to create a sanctuary where people could reconnect with nature without sacrificing comfort.

What started as a single cabin has grown into a beloved retreat, welcoming thousands of guests from around the world. Our commitment to sustainability, local community, and exceptional hospitality remains unchanged.

Every decision we make - from the eco-friendly construction of our cabins to sourcing food from local farmers - reflects our deep respect for the environment and our guests' wellbeing.
