üéØ Site Purpose

The website serves as the official portal for the Inn Among Trees and consists of 4 main pages:

  • Home Page - Welcome page with featured gallery, brief introduction, and call-to-action for bookings
  • About Us - Company history, mission, team profiles, and inn story
  • Services and Experiences - Detailed information about rooms, packages, activities, and additional services
  • Reservations and Contact - Contact forms, booking requests, location information, and contact details

The site aims to attract guests seeking peaceful countryside retreats and provide easy booking capabilities.
